Beginning:

The story began with a simple burst of creativity. It was in this moment Rishabh, Talha, Michael, Mark, and Leon, the founders of Crown began their magnificent journey through the depths of computer science. 24 hours of nonstop learning, vigourous ambition, and brand new coding languages paved the path for this beauty of a dream. The dream of Crown was to create something that would be able to create exposure for those up and coming design artists that could be diamonds in an endless sea. After tireless research and what seemed to be an impossibly crossable path, we have come together to present to you, a taste of royalty.

Rowdy Hacks:

Rowdy Hacks is an organization meant to bring computer programmers from all backgrounds together to create one application that the world may or may not have seen before. Hosted by the University of Texas, Rowdy Hacks 2018 provides the resources for students to display their intuitive perception in the field of computer science in an organized team and pull ideas from one another. The event is intended to provide students with hands on experience in computer applications and introduce them to different career routes awaiting their future.
